Our Motive

The motive of making this web app to track student expenses and sort them category-wise, and set budgets accordingly is to empower students with a tool that can help them manage their finances better. The app aims to provide students with a clear understanding of their financial situation, enabling them to make informed decisions about their spending. By tracking their expenses and setting budgets, students can develop responsible spending habits, avoid overspending, and save money. Ultimately, the goal of the web app is to help students become financially literate and independent, which will serve them well in the long term.
